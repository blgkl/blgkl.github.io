WELCOME TO
ANKARA YILDIRIM BEYAZIT UNIVERSIY CYBER SECURITY STUDENT CLUB

BiltekCyber operates under the Science and Technology Community established at Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University. It consists of a training group offering fundamental cybersecurity education and a Capture The Flag (CTF) team formed to participate in competitions across the country.

BiltekCyber aims to foster knowledge exchange and skill development among its members, equipping them with the tools and expertise necessary to address contemporary digital threats. Through workshops, seminars, and collaborative projects, the community endeavors to cultivate a culture of cybersecurity awareness and proactive defense strategies. Additionally, it serves as a platform for networking and collaboration, facilitating connections between individuals with diverse backgrounds and expertise in the field. Ultimately, the club seeks to contribute to a safer and more secure digital environment for individuals, organizations, and society at large.
