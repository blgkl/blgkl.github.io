VISION

Our vision is to establish a resilient and empowered community of cybersecurity professionals capable of addressing the evolving challenges in the digital landscape. We aspire to cultivate a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, where members are equipped with the knowledge and skills to safeguard digital assets and promote cybersecurity best practices.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide comprehensive cybersecurity education, training, and resources to our members, enabling them to become proficient in identifying, mitigating, and responding to cyber threats. We strive to foster a supportive environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking, empowering individuals to contribute meaningfully to the cybersecurity ecosystem and make a positive impact on the digital world.

A LITTLE HISTORY

BiltekCyber organized a competition called Break The Gleipnir. The Break The Gleipnir adventure, a Capture The Flag competition, took place on August 7, 2021, starting with a webinar consisting of 6 different sessions, featuring guest experts in the field of cybersecurity. The competition lasted for 12 hours with the participation of 128 different teams. While the top-performing teams in BTG were awarded a total prize of 10,000 TL, surprise draws were also held to create an exciting and competitive atmosphere for all participants.
